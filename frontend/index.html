Key Findings

Actionable takeaways from the data analysis.

1

Most players leave early

81% of users churn within 7 days. While normal for free-to-play, this represents a large revenue opportunity. Retained players typically generate 10-50x more value.

Critical
2

Gate at level 30 performs better

gate_30 shows 19.0% Day 7 retention vs 18.2% for gate_40. The earlier friction point may create better pacing. Recommendation: keep gate at level 30.

A/B Result
3

Engagement is the strongest predictor

Players with fewer than 10 rounds churn at 95%+. Those past 50 rounds retain at much higher rates. The model puts 86% feature importance on game rounds.

Insight
4

Day 1 return is make-or-break

Players who do not come back on Day 1 almost never return by Day 7. The first session experience and a compelling reason to return within 24h is critical.

Actionable
5

Improve the first 10 rounds

Add tutorials, rewards, and gentle difficulty in early levels. Push notifications with time-limited bonuses for players who haven't returned after 12 hours.

Recommendation
